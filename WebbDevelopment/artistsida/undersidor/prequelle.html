Ghost

Prequelle

On April 13, 2018, Ghost released a new single, titled "Rats", along with an accompanying music video. This marked the first release from the band, with their "new" frontman Cardinal Copia. Additionally, the band welcomed a new character, saxophonist Papa Nihil, extending Ghost into a septet. The album was titled Prequelle, and it was released on 1 June 2018. A second song from the new album, 'Dance Macabre' was initially released on the band's instagram stories but later that day was made available for streaming in its entirety on 17 May 2018. To promote the album, Ghost began the Rats! on the Road tour in the United States which ran from May 5 until June 1. The band was an opening act for Guns N' Roses in Oslo, Norway on July 19, 2018. Ghost will begin A Pale Tour Named Death in London, England this September at Royal Albert Hall, followed by a North American tour featuring two headlining arena shows in Los Angeles and New York City
